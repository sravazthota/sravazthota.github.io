Telekha is building a powerful social networking platform, which brings the whole world into one platform to communicate, Make friends, share their ideas, technology etc. People use social networking sites for meeting new friends, finding old friends, or locating people who have the same problems or interests they have, called niche networking. More and more relationships and friendships are being formed online and then carried to an offline setting.

Telekha allows registered users to create profiles, upload photos and videos, send messages and keep in touch with friends, family and colleagues. Social media is becoming an integral part of life online as social websites and applications proliferate. Most traditional online media include social components, such as comment, Like, Share fields for users.

Telekha is about conversations, community, connecting with the audience and building relationships. It is not just a broadcast channel or a sales and marketing tool. Authenticity, honesty and open dialogue are key. Telekha not only allows you to hear what people say about you, but enables you to respond.
